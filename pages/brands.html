OUR TOP BRANDS

Fenty Beauty

Founded by Rihanna, Fenty Beauty permanently changed the game with its 'Beauty for All' mission. Celebrated for its revolutionary 50-shade Pro Filt'r foundation range, Fenty ensures that every skin tone is seen and catered to. From the iconic Gloss Bomb that looks good on everyone to the Killawatt highlighters that deliver pure radiance, this brand champions inclusive, high-performance makeup designed for experimentation and self-expression.

Benefit Cosmetics

Benefit Cosmetics believes that laughter is the best cosmetic. Originating from San Francisco, this brand is globally adored for its whimsical packaging and instant beauty solutions. They are the undisputed experts in all things brows, offering cult-favorite products like Gimme Brow+ and Precisely, My Brow Pencil. Beyond brows, their POREfessional primer and Hoola matte bronzer are iconic staples that deliver fast, fabulous results with a playful, fun-loving attitude.

Kiko Milano

Kiko Milano brings the spirit of Italian high fashion and artistry to your makeup bag. This brand masterfully combines premium quality, rich pigmentation, and luxurious textures with an incredibly accessible price point. Kiko is renowned for its vast, ever-changing collections inspired by Milan's runways, offering an unparalleled spectrum of colors. From their best-selling Unlimited Double Touch liquid lipsticks to their vibrant eyeshadow palettes, Kiko empowers you to explore European elegance and creativity without compromise.
