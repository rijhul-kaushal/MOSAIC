Master Contouring

Sculpt and define like a pro using these steps:

  1. Prep & Prime: Start with moisturized skin and primer.
  2. Map Your Face: Identify areas to sculpt (cheekbones, jawline, nose).
  3. Apply Contour: Use a cream or powder 2 shades darker than your skin.
  4. Blend Thoroughly: Use a damp sponge or brush to blend edges seamlessly.
  5. Highlight: Apply highlighter to high points (cheekbones, brow bone).

Perfect Winged Liner

Tips for achieving sharp wings every time:

  1. Steady Hand: Rest your elbow on a surface.
  2. Map the Wing: Draw a small dot where you want the wing to end.
  3. Draw the Line: Connect the dot to the outer corner of your lash line.
  4. Fill It In: Fill the triangle shape created.
  5. Line the Lash Line: Draw liner along your upper lash line to connect.
