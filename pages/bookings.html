Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my appointment?

Just bring yourself! We provide all makeup products, tools, and skincare items. If you have specific products you'd like to use, feel free to bring them.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance, especially for weekend appointments. For special events, book 3-4 weeks ahead.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes! You can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment without any charges. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee.
